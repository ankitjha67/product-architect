## How to Use These References

1. **Before Discovery**: Scan industry-specific apps and competitive products
2. **During Strategy**: Reference business model and growth frameworks
3. **During PRD**: Study how best-in-class products spec similar features (Stripe docs = gold standard)
4. **During Design**: Use design galleries + industry benchmarks for quality calibration
5. **During Engineering**: Reference architecture patterns for similar-scale products
6. **During Security**: Cross-check against OWASP, PCI-DSS, and relevant regulatory docs
7. **During Launch**: Study growth frameworks and analytics best practices

**The goal is not to copy — it's to understand what "great" looks like in each domain
so your product meets that bar from day one.**
